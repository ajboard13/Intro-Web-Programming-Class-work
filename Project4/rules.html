Rules

All contests must have a set of rules.

November 1, 2015 Aaron Board

The rules for this contest are as follows:

  • No Nudity
  • No Profanity
  • No Stealing other people's work
  • Must be submited as a PNG
  • Only one submission per topic, per person

You can submit whatever photo you wish as long as they follow these guidelines. I want this to be a family friedly site, so if your photo is inappropriate, please do not submit it. I did leave out the most important rule from this list, that rule is to HAVE FUN!
